# Superstore-Business-Dashboard
🛒 Superstore Business Insight Dashboard

📌 Project Overview
The Superstore Business Insight Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales, profit, customer behavior, regional performance, product performance, and city-wise sales. The dashboard helps business managers identify trends, improve profitability, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Business Problem
The company wants to understand:

Which regions generate the highest sales and profit?
Which products perform the best?
Which cities contribute the most revenue?
How do discounts affect profit?
What business strategies can improve overall performance?
🎯 Project Objective

Develop an interactive dashboard to

Monitor key business KPIs
Analyze sales trends over time
Compare regional performance
Evaluate product profitability
Identify top-performing cities
Support business decision-making

💡 Business Recommendations

Increase focus on high-profit products.
Optimize discount strategies.
Improve customer retention.
